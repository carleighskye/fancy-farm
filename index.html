About us

Fancy Farm is a family-owned and run farm and apple orchard in Harrisonburg, VA. We also grow a variety of vegetables and fruits that are available for purchase in our Farm Store!

What we offer

  • Apple Picking
  • Hayrides
  • Animal Petting Zoo
  • Food and drinks

Events

Every Saturday starting the first weekend of September until Thanksgiving, we host local businesses and have a fall fair at the farm. There is live music, games, giveaways, and more!

Pet Policy

Due to the Department of Agriculture's Good Food Policy, we can no longer allow dogs to visit our Farm. Service dogs are trained to work or perform a task for persons with disabilities. Comfort and/or emotional support animals are not service animals, and are not recognized by the ADA, and as such are not allowed. We thank you for your understanding.
